More Than Just a Screening

📺

Premium Screens

Large-format high-definition displays strategically positioned so every seat in the venue gets the best view of the action.

Bottle Service

Reserve your section with bottle service starting at $250. Premium spirits, Champagne, and signature cocktail packages tailored to your squad.

Global Crowd

Atlanta's most diverse crowd comes together at Josephine. Cheer with fans from Ghana, Nigeria, Brazil, Morocco, France, Mexico and beyond.
